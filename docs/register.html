CMW Fees and Guest Meal Tickets

 

  • Fees include meal tickets for the three group meals, receptions, all CMW activities, and use of the chamber music library and the building facilities.

  • CMW alumni include anyone who has previously attended Chamber Music Weekend.
  • If financial difficulties would keep you from attending, please contact us. Some financial assistance is available.
  • Space is limited!  Please register early!
